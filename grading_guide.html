This page features a guide to determine the quality of the golf ball

There are 4 tiers:

  • Grade A
  • Grade B
  • Grade C
  • Rejects

Grade A

Best quality - no markings, or very little cuts

Grade B

Medium quality - small, scrapes cuts

Grade C

Large scrapes which may affect quality of play

Rejects

Ball is only suitable for use in practice, cheapest balls, low quality
